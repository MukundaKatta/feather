# Feather

> The CRM built for a team of one.

Freelancers, consultants, solo founders. Track clients, deals, and follow-ups without Salesforce-level pain.

## What you get

- **Contacts that remember** — Last conversation, birthday, kids' names, what they invoice. All one click away.
- **Nudges when it matters** — Haven't spoken to your best client in six weeks? We'll tell you. Gently.
- **Pipeline at a glance** — Six deals in flight, three at risk, ten in onboarding. See it all on one screen.

## Category

Productivity. Part of a 50-product exploration of high-demand consumer and SMB markets.
